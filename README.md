### Business Problem:
Olist, a platform that connects retailers of various sizes to the largest marketplaces in Brazil, seeks to optimize its commercial and logistics operations to strengthen profitability and the end-customer experience. The management team observed significant variations in delivery efficiency between different product categories and seller profiles, which directly impacts the perceived value of the brand. They are particularly interested in understanding how seller shipping times, shipping conditions, and payment facilities (such as installments) drive sales volume and customer loyalty. It is necessary to analyze the company's transaction and logistics dataset to answer the following central business question: "How can Olist use sales data, delivery times, and customer reviews to reduce delay rates, identify high-performing categories, and optimize the relationship between seller efficiency and customer satisfaction?"
### Deliverables:
- Rapid data exploration and ETL (Python): Clean and transform the raw datasets for analysis;
- Data Analysis (SQL): Organize the data into a structured format in MySQL, defining primary and foreign keys across the 8 tables. Execute 16 queries to answer the defined business questions, covering metrics for logistics, sales, customer satisfaction, and seller performance;
- Visualization and Insights (Power BI): Organize the data into a structured format in MySQL, defining primary and foreign keys across the 8 tables. Execute 16 queries to answer the defined business questions, covering metrics for logistics, sales, customer satisfaction, and seller performance — each query exported as a View for direct consumption by Power BI;
- Reporting and Presentation: Write a clear project report summarizing the main findings and business recommendations.
